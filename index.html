About Norrtälje

Welcome to Roslagen's capital Norrtälje! Norrtälje is an idyllic small coastal town with authentic original buildings and a medieval road network with cobbled streets, combined with a new modern harbour area with scenic architecture and beautiful views. You will find quaint shops and larger chain stores. An abundance of restaurants and cafes, museums, events, activities and more! Combined with its close location to Stockholm, whether you are looking for a wonderful day trip, a relaxing weekend or a longer holiday, Norrtälje is a truly wonderful place to visit.
